What is Machine Learning?

What you call AI is actually Machine Learning. Machine Learning is not the brain of AI but it is a part of it. It is a way to teach computers to learn from examples and data instead of following exact instructions. By analyzing patterns in data, a computer can make predictions, recognize things, or solve problems on its own, even in situations it hasn’t seen before.
